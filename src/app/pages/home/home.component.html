ABOUT OMAR

My journey with UX/UI design all started at 6 years old, when I started playing with a Nintendo 64 game console. While playing Legend of Zelda, I was captivated by… I brought this fascination with videogaming to life by starting to learn 3D design with Rhino 3D and pursuing an architecture degree. Throughout my studies, I realized that my architectural skills were similar to web designers and content creators. Successful architects, web designers, and content creators embody creativity and think about how end users interact with your creation. Instead of pursuing architecture from a traditional sense, designing buildings, I wanted to apply my experience to creating a polished online platform for my clients.

5 years ago, I took the plunge and became a freelance UX/UI designer. I began collaborating with up-and-coming brands and companies who needed quality UX/UI design to showcase my skills of delivering high stand pieces. From concept all the way to prototyping, these projects built my portfolio and expertise in app development, branding, and creating a seamless user experience through compelling designs and images.

To me, UX/UI design is more than creating a functional or practical interface for users. It’s about creating an eye-catching piece of art, fostering a familiar and enjoyable user experience, and expanding accessibility through innovation and creativity. My favorite part about being a UX/UI designer is connecting with motivated people who are excited to share their ideas with the world and collaborating in a fast-paced environment to deliver high-impact results.
